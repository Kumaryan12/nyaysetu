NALSA Legal Aid Basic Information

The National Legal Services Authority, also known as NALSA, provides legal services information and access to legal aid through legal services institutions.
Legal aid is intended to help people who may not be able to afford legal services or who are otherwise eligible under legal services law and schemes.
A person seeking legal aid may approach the appropriate Legal Services Authority. Depending on the situation, this may include the District Legal Services Authority, State Legal Services Authority, Taluk Legal Services Committee, High Court Legal Services Committee, or Supreme Court Legal Services Committee.
NyayaSetu should explain that it is not a substitute for a lawyer. Users should contact the appropriate Legal Services Authority or a qualified lawyer for case-specific legal help.
For urgent safety or violence-related situations, users should contact appropriate emergency or local authorities immediately.
