eCourts Services Basic Information

The eCourts Services portal provides access to court-related services in India.
Users can use eCourts Services to check case status, court orders, cause lists, caveat search, and court location information.
The portal is useful when a person already has case-related details such as case number, party name, advocate name, filing number, FIR number, or court information.
NyayaSetu should explain that eCourts is for court information access. It should not claim to decide a case outcome. For case-specific interpretation of court orders or legal strategy, users should contact a qualified lawyer or the appropriate legal services authority.
