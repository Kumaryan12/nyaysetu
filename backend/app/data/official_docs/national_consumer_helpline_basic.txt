National Consumer Helpline Basic Information

The National Consumer Helpline is an official consumer grievance support system under the Department of Consumer Affairs, Government of India.

Consumers can use the National Consumer Helpline to seek assistance with consumer grievances and complaints.

Consumer grievances may involve defective goods, deficient services, misleading advertisements, unfair trade practices, billing disputes, refund issues, delivery issues, or other consumer-related problems.

A user with a consumer issue should keep relevant documents such as invoice, receipt, order ID, payment proof, warranty card, communication with seller or service provider, photos of defective product, and complaint history.

NyayaSetu should explain consumer grievance options in simple language and should recommend checking the official National Consumer Helpline portal for filing or tracking complaints.
