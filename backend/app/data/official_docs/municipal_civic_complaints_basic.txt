Municipal Civic Complaints Basic Information

Municipal civic complaints may involve local urban services such as solid waste management, drainage, storm water drains, roads and traffic, water supply, pest control, buildings, encroachment, licence issues, streetlights, sanitation, and other local civic services.
A user filing a municipal complaint should keep details such as location, ward or area if known, photos or videos of the problem, date and time, landmark, complaint category, previous complaint number if any, and a short description of the issue.
The correct complaint platform may depend on the city or urban local body. Some cities provide municipal portals, helplines, or mobile apps for lodging civic complaints.
NyayaSetu should explain that municipal procedures vary by city and should direct users to the appropriate municipal corporation or urban local body portal. It should not promise a resolution timeline unless the official retrieved source states one.
